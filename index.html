Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about creating your activities.

Is GenPrintable really free?

Yes! Our core generators for dot-to-dots, name tracing, and mazes are 100% free for both personal home use and classroom use. We believe in making education accessible to everyone.

Can I use this for my kindergarten class?

Absolutely. Many of our users are teachers! Our tools are designed to align with early childhood learning standards, specifically focusing on fine motor skills, letter recognition, and number sequencing.

Do I need to create an account?

No account is required for the free tools. You can generate and download PDFs instantly as a guest. However, creating an account allows you to save your previous designs.

What format are the downloads?

All activities are generated as high-resolution A4 PDFs, optimized for standard home and office printers to ensure crisp lines and clear text.
