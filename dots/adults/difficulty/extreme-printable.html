Why are the dot paths often nonlinear?

In high-density puzzles, dot paths must sometimes weave to ensure maximum image detail retention, adding to the extreme difficulty. This is necessary to render complex curves and subtle shading.

What is the best way to print these dense puzzles?

Always use the high-resolution PDF files. Ensure your printer quality setting is set to "Best" or "Photo" on plain white paper to ensure the tiny dot numbers are crisp and legible.
