Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 3, 2025

1 Introduction

GenPrintable.com is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our services, including our AI generation tools and printable resources. Our core principle is minimal collection of Personally Identifiable Information (PII).

2 Information We Collect

We primarily collect Non-Personally Identifiable Information (Non-PII):

  • Usage Data: This includes pages viewed, session duration, browser type, and interaction data with our generators (e.g., the parameters you input into the AI Cut and Paste Generator).
  • Technical Information: IP address (used for geolocation and abuse prevention), and device identifiers.
  • Account Information (Optional): If you register an account or purchase AI enhanced features, we collect your email address. Payment details are handled by secure third-party processors (e.g., Stripe) and are not stored by us.

3 Third-Party Advertising

Mandatory Disclosure for AdSense Compliance

GenPrintable.com utilizes Google AdSense to serve advertisements. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites.

Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to GenPrintable.com and other sites on the Internet.

Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google Ad and Content Network privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

We also use Google Analytics to analyze site usage. These third-party services may collect information about your usage and combine it with other information to provide advertising services.

  • Third-Party Links: We strongly advise you to consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices.
  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4 Paid Services Data Handling

GenPrintable.com offers AI enhanced features (e.g., higher resolution outputs, batch generation). Purchase and subscription information is processed by third-party secure payment platforms. We only retain records of your purchase history and your current AI service credit/subscription status.

5 Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Our services are designed for educational and entertainment purposes but are generally directed towards adults (parents, teachers) for use.

We do not knowingly collect Personally Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13 in compliance with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with PII without your consent, please notify us immediately via the contact email and we will take steps to remove that information.

6 Your Choices and Rights

You can choose to disable cookies through your individual browser options. Disabling cookies may affect your ability to use certain features on GenPrintable.com (such as saving generation preferences).
