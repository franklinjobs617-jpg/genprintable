Expert Tips: Guiding Fine Motor Skills

"I always tell parents to focus on the starting point of the letter first, not the finish line. Don't worry about perfection; focus on the directionality. The Dot-to-Dot Lines for Tracing help immensely by making the path obvious."

— Occupational Therapy Specialist

How to Hold the Crayon: Ensure a dynamic tripod grip—this is key for transitioning from scribbling to writing. Use crayons broken into smaller pieces to naturally encourage the correct grasp.
